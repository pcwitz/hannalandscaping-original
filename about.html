About Adam, the Landscaper

Adam holds an Associates Degree in Horticulture from the University of Cincinnati. He is pulling from over 15 years of landscaping experience. He's an expert in plant hardiness zones 5-7, spanning the Midwest to the Northeastern United States. In his native region of Cincinnati, he is versed in foliage species and refers to them both by their common botanical names and their Latin equivalents.

Hanna Landscaping and Design is a complete design, installation, and landscape management service for the Greater Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky area.
